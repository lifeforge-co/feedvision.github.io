Overview

FeedVision is an Instagram feed planner for iOS that helps you visualize and organize your Instagram grid before posting. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data FeedVision accesses, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

The short version: FeedVision processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external server. No analytics. No tracking. No ads.

1. Information We Access

When you connect your Instagram account, FeedVision accesses the following through Instagram's official APIs:

Instagram Account Data

  • Your Instagram username, display name, biography, and profile picture
  • Your follower count, following count, and post count
  • Your published Instagram posts and their associated images

Public Business & Creator Profiles

  • Publicly available profile information from business and creator accounts via Instagram's Business Discovery API
  • Only data that is already publicly visible on Instagram is accessed

2. How We Access Your Data

FeedVision connects to Instagram using OAuth 2.0 via the Instagram Graph API. This is the official, secure authentication method provided by Meta. You authorize the connection directly through Instagram's login flow — FeedVision never sees or stores your Instagram password.

3. How Data Is Stored

All data remains on your device. Nothing is sent to Lifeforge or any third-party server.

Data Storage Method Location
Instagram access token iOS Keychain Encrypted On device
Account profile info SwiftData (local database) On device
Instagram photos Local image cache On device
Planned posts & grid layout SwiftData (local database) On device

Keychain Security: Your Instagram access token is stored in the iOS Keychain, which uses hardware-backed encryption provided by the Secure Enclave on your device.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not send your data to any third-party servers
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information
  • We do not store your Instagram password
  • We do not post to Instagram on your behalf without explicit action
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data

5. Third-Party Services

FeedVision communicates only with Instagram's official APIs (operated by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to retrieve your account data and photos. No other third-party services are used.

Your use of Instagram is subject to Instagram's Terms of Use and Instagram's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times.

To delete your data:

  • Sign out within FeedVision to remove your access token and cached data
  • Delete the app from your device to remove all stored data, including the local database and image cache
  • Revoke access in your Instagram settings under Settings > Security > Apps and Websites to disconnect FeedVision from your account

Because no data is stored on our servers, there is no server-side data to request deletion of.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because FeedVision stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to any server, most GDPR provisions are inherently satisfied by our architecture. Nonetheless, we recognize the following rights:

  • Right of Access — All your data is on your device and accessible to you at any time.
  • Right to Rectification — You can update your data by re-syncing with Instagram or editing planned posts.
  • Right to Erasure — Delete the app or sign out to erase all data. No server-side data exists to erase.
  • Right to Data Portability — Your data lives on your device in standard formats.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — You may revoke Instagram access or delete the app at any time.
  • Right to Object — No automated profiling or marketing processing occurs.

Legal Basis for Processing: FeedVision processes data based on your explicit consent, granted when you authenticate your Instagram account through the OAuth flow. You may withdraw consent at any time by signing out or revoking access.

Data Controller: Lifeforge (lifeforge.co). For GDPR-related inquiries, contact us at support@lifeforge.co.

8. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information.

  • Right to Know — FeedVision accesses Instagram profile data and photos as described in Section 1. All data remains on your device.
  • Right to Delete — Delete the app to remove all data. No data exists on our servers.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale — We do not sell your personal information. Ever.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — You will not receive different service for exercising your privacy rights.

Categories of personal information collected: Instagram account identifiers and media content, stored locally on your device only.

Categories of personal information sold or disclosed for business purposes: None.

9. Children's Privacy

FeedVision is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Use of FeedVision requires an Instagram account, which itself requires users to meet minimum age requirements.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed by FeedVision:

  • Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain, protected by hardware encryption
  • All communication with Instagram's API is conducted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers, eliminating network-based attack vectors
  • The app leverages iOS platform security, including App Sandbox and Data Protection

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
